ABOUT US

White Arch Studio is an interior design firm which focuses on providing high quality designs which are simple yet aesthetic. We specialise in designing residential and commercial spaces. Founded in 2022 by Izaaz Mohammad, believes in the minimalistic yet luxury looking style. We are also willing to collaborate with individuals or organisations for drawings, 3D views and layouts. We are very proficient in handing over the project before the time bound.
