L.I.C Office and Art Studio, Dadar

This happens to be our first commercial project which is a combination of a commercial L.I.C. office and a professional art studio. While designing, we thought of dividing the entire space into two sections. The first section near the entrance is our office. A desk, work computer and client space is all adjusted in such a compact space. The second section is the art gallery where paintings and sketches are displayed and created. It is a bliss seeing a commercial environment blending with an artist's space.
